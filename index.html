Who I am

Nice to meet you!

I am Karen Mannheim. A mom, entrepreneur, and a Lighting Design expert born and raised in Lima.


In 1992 I founded TRAZZO, the leading lighting design, automation and home furniture in Perú. With over 30 years of experience and over 500 projects across Latam, US & Middle East, I’m lucky enough to say: I love helping people like you to truly live and enjoy their spaces to the fullest.
